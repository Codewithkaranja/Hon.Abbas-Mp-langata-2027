Biography

Hon. Abbas Ibrahim Khalif is a visionary leader, philanthropist, and successful entrepreneur who has redefined urban representation in Nairobi. Currently serving as the Member of County Assembly (MCA) for South C, he has earned a reputation as a "man of the people" through his hands-on approach to infrastructure, security, and youth empowerment.

Having transitioned from a prominent career as a business mogul, Abbas understands the importance of economic stability and private-sector efficiency. His entry into politics was driven by a desire to bring professional management to public office, ensuring that the resources of Lang'ata benefit every resident regardless of their background.

As he prepares for his 2027 parliamentary bid, Abbas Khalif remains focused on his "Action, Not Words" philosophy. Through the Abass Foundation, he has already begun laying the groundwork for a more prosperous Lang'ata by investing in grassroots talent, modernizing educational facilities, and championing for a cleaner, safer environment.
