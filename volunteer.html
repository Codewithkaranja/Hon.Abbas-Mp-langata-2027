Volunteer Opportunities

Choose how you'd like to contribute to Lang'ata's transformation

Campaign Outreach

Help us connect with voters across Lang'ata's constituencies through door-to-door campaigns, community meetings, and public forums.

  • Door-to-door canvassing
  • Community mobilization
  • Voter registration drives
  • Public awareness campaigns

Event Coordination

Help organize and manage campaign events, rallies, town halls, and community meetings across Lang'ata Constituency.

  • Event planning & logistics
  • Venue coordination
  • Crowd management
  • Guest registration

Digital & Social Media

Use your digital skills to amplify our message online and engage with voters through social media platforms.

  • Social media management
  • Content creation
  • Digital campaigning
  • Online community building

Phone Banking

Reach out to voters via phone calls to share our vision, answer questions, and mobilize support for Abbas Khalif.

  • Voter outreach calls
  • Information dissemination
  • Follow-up with supporters
  • Feedback collection

Administrative Support

Help with the day-to-day operations of the campaign office and ensure smooth coordination of activities.

  • Office administration
  • Data entry & management
  • Volunteer coordination
  • Resource distribution

Logistics & Transportation

Support campaign activities by assisting with transportation, logistics, and movement of materials across Lang'ata.

  • Campaign material distribution
  • Transport coordination
  • Logistics planning
  • Equipment management
